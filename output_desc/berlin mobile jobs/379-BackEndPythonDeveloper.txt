About the job
ABOUT THE ROLE

We are looking for a Back-End Python Developer to join the development group in
building and improving the SaaS platform that fights climate change with code. . As a BE Developer you will be part of a team of 5 to 10 talented colleagues and work at designing and implementing (and also testing, monitoring, debugging, refactoring, optimizing) the best platform to manage Clean Energy assets in the industry. You will be using latest technologies to do so.
As a Developer, you will also have an important role to play within your team. You will be
able to leverage each and every one of your skills to make your team achieve its goals.
It could be either by mentoring others, strategize to maximize the value delivered,
getting new knowledge and share it, bring energy, identify and reflect on team’s issue,
contribute to continuous improvement or generate fun.
This platform, helps managing renewable energy plants, supporting the business of the customers. It also collects data points from several sources, cleanses it, detects occurring issues, and uses it to help manage thousands of renewable energy plants all over the world. Also adds the toolset needed to take make sure the plants are running smoothly, and that it can be profitable.
The offices are in Munich, Germany, but you’ll be working remotely. They're an international team spread throughout the world (US, Canada, Philippines, Sweden, Spain, Germany and Greece).
