About the job

Description

Our Team is looking for an Android Automotive OS applications developer (m/f/d) that is ready to proactively function in a cross functional agile team and focus on development and integration of Android software on an Android Automotive OS-based Infotainment Platform, analyzing performance issues and performing fault tracing of the in-vehicle software and supporting systems.

In this exciting position you will have the chance to develop your skillset by integrating Android OS on the next generation of vehicle computers powering car infotainment systems for passenger cars.

Responsibilities

You are involved in the development of the best-in-class next generation of Android-based infotainment software in close cooperation with our automotive customers
You are responsible for Android development with a focus on styling and theming of apps
You test your software not only on the PC, but also in one of our customers test vehicles
You work with modern and fast infrastructures in the form of Git and Jenkins, among other things, allow you an uninterrupted development flow and prompt feedback through fast build times

Requirements

Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Communication Technology or a comparable qualification
Professional Experience with In-Vehicle Infotainment Systems
Knowledge in of widget libraries for a modern UI development language
Experience with a programming language such as Java, C# or Python and a good understanding of programming in general (OOP, unit tests)
Passion for UI design details (color, font, icon, positioning) as well as Android and automotive software solutions
English level: Full professional

Nice to have

German level: Upper-Intermediate+ (B2+)
