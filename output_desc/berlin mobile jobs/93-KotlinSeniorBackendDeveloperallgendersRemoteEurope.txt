About the job
Company Description

upday is a media and tech company and the leading news app in Europe with more than 25 million MAUs.

Founded in 2016 as a joint project between Axel Springer and Samsung Electronics, upday became profitable after just 3 years in the market.

If you’re looking to join a dynamic and international team full of fantastic talent that has the energy of a start-up but the stability of an established media company - look no further, we are your match!

You will be doing:

🚀 Develop our features in pair-programming sessions, followed by code reviews

🚀 Build Microservices which are written in Kotlin, running in Kubernetes and hosted on AWS

🚀 Enrich the backend team and raise it to a higher level

🚀 Manage many varied and exciting projects and work with external, interesting and international clients on enabling to realise their products


You are bringing:

👩‍💻 You have a good understanding of the entire lifecycle of building services with clients. This means responsibility for engaging and improving the whole lifecycle of services from inception and design, through deployment and operation

👩‍💻 You love software that is well crafted; You are excited about what you plan to build with your team and you actively own your career always thinking about the next step to bring it forward

👩‍💻 You have an eye for software design and can talk eloquently on a range of topics due to your experience and also from reading and experimentation

👩‍💻 You have 6+ years of work experience in building B2B products and B2C applications - production-ready applications would be a plus

About our Backend-Team:

The backend team is self-organizing and highly autonomous working with agile methodology driven by our company OKR’s. You’ll be working directly with our Product Owners and stakeholders in a collaborative and cross-functional manner.

This approach requires a huge amount of teamwork and is not always perfect. We believe in the quality of the services which we build but in case something goes wrong we have engineers on-call. Generally, we are solving problems and making decisions as a team. If you have working experience building production-ready applications, great! We want to know more.

Which frameworks do you use? How do you test your applications? What patterns and technologies do you use to make these apps failure tolerant, scalable, reliable and resilient? We are highly interested in the problems you faced. The more the better!
