About The Role

We are looking for a junior engineer to join the Vault Core team and focus on the tools and features that enhance integration with third-party services and partners. You will help design, prototype, and implement features to interface with a variety of external systems, and enhance the extensibility support within Vault.

This person will be a part of the Vault engineering team at HashiCorp, working to provide a fantastic and broadly used secrets tool for businesses and individuals handling all sizes and types of infrastructure.

You can learn more about the Vault product with our Co-Founder & CTO here: Introduction to HashiCorp Vault with Armon Dadgar

In This Role, You Can Expect To

Design and build plugins and tools integrating Vault with a wide variety of third-party systems.
Help contribute to the Vault community by providing code review, mentorship, and support to HashiCorp employees, community members, and partners
Advocate and support improvements to Vault APIs and core to improve development and integration of tools and plugins
Work on issues and improvements critical to the success of HashiCorp customers and the broader community

You may be a good fit for our team if:

You have experience in a programming language like Go, Java, or Python
You like writing programs that can be understood by their users
Experience with Kubernetes or other container orchestration systems.
Experience working with distributed software within a Linux environment
Experience with customer support or OSS community support to help work with the Vault community
Expertise in one or more of the major public clouds
Familiar with infrastructure management concepts
Working understanding of commonly-used security technologies
Experience using Vault, Azure and GCP
