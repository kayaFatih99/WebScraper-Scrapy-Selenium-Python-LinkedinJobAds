We are looking to hire a skilled web developer that will be focused on back-end development and front-end delivery of a dynamic web experience. What you'll do :

Work closely with team of designers and developers to build an engaging website experience leveraging mobile-first and responsive design best practices.
Proficiency with WordPress, PHP, AWS, and Pantheon to run the global Criteo.com property.
Management of site themes and plugins.
Develop, deploy and enhance the website experience with a PHP-based content management system including database management and server integration.
Analyze marketing plans to identify how the website can deliver the optimal user experience.
Ensure website quality and compliance measures for cookie controls and visitor privacy.
Experience managing web servers to maintain the site infrastructure from DNS, CDN, development environments to global hosting and distribution of content.

Proficiency in HTML5, CSS, PHP, JavaScript, GIT and databases.

Good communication skills by taking complex setups and making them simple.
