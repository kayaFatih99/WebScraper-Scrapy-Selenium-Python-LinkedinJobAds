About the job
What about us?

Sunhero is a residential solar energy company on a mission to make access to clean, PV solar energy easy and affordable. We leverage technology and innovation to accelerate the transition to clean energy.

Sunhero was founded in 2021 by two entrepreneurs with over 25 years of experience launching companies in the international markets, including Airbnb, Rover, and Course Hero. Sunhero is headquartered in Berlin, Germany, and Barcelona, Spain. We are VC-backed by leading European investors, including Speedinvest and All Iron Ventures, and have ambitious growth plans.

We started in Europe, but climate change knows no borders. And neither will we. Want to know more? Please visit Sunhero.com

What would you do working with us?

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our team who is motivated to work on using technology to help solve the world's most pressing problem.

To succeed in this role, you need to be an entrepreneur. You are analytical and strategic, with a pragmatic sense of how to get things done and openness for iterations and changes of course. You know that building a company means doing whatever works best for customers and partners and you are willing to roll up your sleeves to make this happen.

- Design client-side and server-side architecture.
- Collaborate with our development team to efficiently develop new features and fix ongoing issues.
- Analyse, model, and build responsive web applications.
- Write clean and maintainable code that is easy to understand and reuse.
- Test software to ensure responsiveness and efficiency.
- Be able to autonomously work on all stages of software development; model, implementation, and delivery.
- Tackle new challenges and learn new technologies without being afraid to make mistakes along the way.
- Be able to establish new architecture guidelines to create sustainable architecture patterns and guide others to utilize them.
- Actively and constantly works with the team to improve their workflow processes to increase their outcome.
- Guide less experienced peers through difficult challenges.
- Be proactive while developing and take responsibility for the projects you are working on.
- Multiply the skills of other team members by sharing knowledge.
