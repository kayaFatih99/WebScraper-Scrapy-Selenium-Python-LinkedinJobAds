About the job
Company description
We are a social networking app dedicated to creating positive change. Based in the UK, Spain and Morocco, our company was founded with the mission of empowering users to create social impact on the issues that matter most to them (minority rights, environmental causes, etc.)

About the role
We are looking for a Lead Full-Stack Mobile App Developer to produce scalable software solutions. You’ll be responsible for the software development life cycle, from conception to deployment. As a Full Stack Developer, you should be comfortable around both front-end and back-end coding languages, development frameworks and third-party libraries. You should also be a team player with a knack for visual design and utility.

If you’re also familiar with Agile methodologies, we’d like to meet you.

This is a remote role. You will initially work with us on a freelance basis, with the possibility of joining the team on a permanent basis after an initial project phase of 3-4 months.
