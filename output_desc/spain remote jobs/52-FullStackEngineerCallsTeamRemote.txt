Mattermost is seeking an experienced full stack Software Engineer to join the Calls team and help bring voice and screen sharing to the Mattermost Suite. Built using WebRTC, Mattermost Calls integrates with the existing Channels, Boards and Playbooks products to allow both self-hosted and Cloud customers to securely communicate with each other on a platform they can control.
