Job Overview
We are looking for a skilled full stack developer to support the efforts of the Data Department. In this role, you will be required to design, write and maintain code for our current and future projects.
The role requires an individual who is passionate about web applications, cloud computing and data. It is a great opportunity for someone to join a team that is transforming itself into making decisions in a data driven way.

Developing web apps (front-end & back-end)
Designing and developing APIs
Meeting both technical and consumer needs,
Staying abreast of developments in web applications and programming languages.

Familiarity with database technology such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle

Familiarity with Linux servers for deployment such as RHEL, CentOS and Ubuntu,
