About the job
We are looking for a forward-thinking Senior Mobile Engineer to join our Product Engineering team, working directly with software engineers, QA engineers, Product managers, designers and more, as well as an open landscape to all parts and people across our organisation.

We believe in ownership, and want to empower our experts in utilising their skill sets in a collaborative and supportive agile working environment that is focused on developing our product to the next level.

We have a wealth of opportunities and problems to solve. If you are looking to make an impact; to be part of a team where your thoughts are heard and given space to develop, as well as the autonomy to thrive in a role, we want to hear from you.

Key Tasks and Responsibilities will be to:
Lead projects through an end-to-end design process: from user research, ux concept, strategy, application architecture, interaction design, launch and testing
Contribute to existing products and develop new functionalities
Leading technical design changes displaying technical ownership and employing best practices. Continuously improve our working processes
Collaborate with user researchers, product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to frame user problems and take ideas from ideation through implementation
