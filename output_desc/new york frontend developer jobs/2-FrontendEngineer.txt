About the job
Our client is looking for Frontend Developers with a keen eye for design interested in joining a world-class team. You'll be working closely with backend engineers, product, and design teams to ship a core product that will define the future of the business.

There is a significant growth opportunity for the Engineering team as the company continues to excel and invest in technology.

This is a full-time and not a contract or C2C role.

Responsibilities
Develop and maintain pixel-perfect, cross-browser friendly, responsive websites.
Work alongside Backend Developers, Graphic Designers, and User Experience Designers to ensure all elements of web creation are consistent.
Work with QA on developing testing processes, frameworks, and automation strategies.
Diagnose, troubleshoot, and resolve compatibility issues.
Strike a balance between functional and aesthetic design.
Create rapid prototypes and help build capabilities by working on extra projects.

Qualifications
Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related major.
3+ years of experience in developing software applications.
Proficiency in Javascript, HTML/CSS, and modern web technologies.
Experience with one or more modern frontend frameworks such as React or Angular.
Experience with graphic design applications such as Adobe Illustrator.
The desire to work at an early-stage startup and have significant product ownership.
